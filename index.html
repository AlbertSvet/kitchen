Russian cuisine

Natural features of places of residence

Extensive forests and long winters - determined many of the characteristics of traditional cuisine Very common hot soups, as they can keep you warm in cold weather. In central Russia, frosts reach -30 -35 degrees, so hot soup will not hurt you

A variety of hot soups and porridges, pickles and pickles, mushroom dishes and dough dishes

Borsch, Buckwheat porridge, Sauerkraut, Pickled mushrooms, Dumplings, Manti

Traditional Russian dishes

Solyanka

Cooked in meat broth. Sour-salty foods must be added to the broth: olive cucumbers, lemon, pickled cucumber brine, tomato paste.Sausages potatoes.

    Products:
  • Beef on the bone
  • Smoked sausage
  • Sausages
  • Pickled cucumbers
  • Onion
  • Pitted olives
  • Tomato paste
  • Bay leaf
  • Salt
Okroshka

This is a cold soup made from vegetables, pickles, meat cut into small cubes. In okroshka, boiled beef, chicken or sausages are used. Add fresh or pickled cucumbers, boiled potatoes. Put the greens, close the lid and place in a cold place for at least 30 minutes. Before serving, the dish is poured with kefir and tan

    Products:
  • Beef (boiled)
  • Chicken egg
  • Cucumber
  • Radish
  • Greens (onion, dill, parsley)
  • Tan
  • Kefir
BORSCH

Traditional borsch is a soup based on beef or pork broth with the addition of vegetables - beets, cabbage, carrots and potatoes. Borscht is served with dressing, usually sour cream. In addition, rye bread and lard are served with borscht.

    Products:
  • Beef
  • Beets
  • Potatoes
  • White cabbage
  • Carrot
  • Onion
  • Tomato paste
  • Vinegar
  • Bay leaf
  • Black peppercorns
  • Dill and / or parsley (for serving) - 3-4 sprigs
Dumplings

They were first prepared in the Urals more than 600 years ago. The shape of the dumplings resembles an auricle - from this similarity the name originated, meaning "bread ear". The recipe is simple - a mixture of ground beef, lamb and chicken, wrapped in an envelope of unleavened dough. A dense dough is kneaded on the basis of eggs, flour and water. Dumplings are boiled in boiling salted water, where black peppercorns and a couple of bay leaves are added. Served with or without broth. Mustard, sour cream, butter are used as seasonings and sauces. In everyday life, dumplings are prepared by the whole family.

    Products:
  • Egg
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Wheat flour
  • ground beef
  • ground pork
  • large onion
  • garlic clove
  • black pepper

Pancakes

The Mongols and Buryats consider themselves the ancestors of this dish. Even under Genghis Khan, they had their own recipe for pancakes called gambir, and they are very zealous in defending their right to this invention.

    Products:
  • milk
  • eggs
  • flour
  • Butter
  • sugar
  • salt
Salad Olivie

Olivier is a Russian cuisine appetizer salad made from boiled root vegetables, pickles, eggs with meat or boiled sausage in mayonnaise dressing. According to the generally accepted version, it bears the name of the author of the recipe - French chef Lucien Olivier (1838-1883)

    Products:
  • boiled potatoes
  • hard boiled eggs
  • boiled carrot
  • boiled sausage "Doctor's"
  • pickled cucumbers
  • canned green peas
  • mayonnaise
  • parsley and dill leaves optional salt
